Detailed Description

template<size_t N_vars, size_t N_eq, size_t N_ineq>
class ActiveSetSolver< N_vars, N_eq, N_ineq >

Primal Active-Set QP Solver.

J(u) = 1/2 u^T P u + q^T u subject to A_eq u = b_eq A_ineq u <= b_ineq
